Who Is Coaching For?

Coaching is for everyone; it really depends upon the individual and where they are in life.

We face life changes every day. Sometimes it is age-related like transitioning from a busy parent to an empty-nester, or handling the thought of our mortality.

Sometimes it’s from loss like divorce, death of a loved one, or even one’s job.

What is The Process Like?

After a brief Skype conversation we will decide our next course of action. Based on your free 30-minute session, I can recommend the best coaching package which has provided results using my proven systematic approach to unlocking the barriers in your life journey. I am here for you.

1. Free 30-minute Skype Session

All it takes is a 30 minute consult to decide if we are compatible and can work together. Please use my contact form.

2. Make a decision

During your free 30-minute session, I will recommend the best coaching package based on my experience and systematic approach in helping people. After that, the decision is yours to select a coaching package or one-time session.

3. Select your coaching package

After our quick session, you have the option to choose which coaching package best fits your needs and budget.

4. Schedule your dates and time

Consistency is key. Whether you choose the 4, 7, or 11 week package, I recommend choosing the same day and time each week.

About Me

A certified NLP practitioner, Colin Porter became interested in the spiritual side of life after his own life-changing experience, which led to a keen interest in mindset and our connection to spirit.

After a brief term as a medium on the spiritualist church circuit, he expanded his quest for knowledge to go beyond the why of mindset and delved deeper in to the HOW of things; reverse engineering how people accept, cope, and move forward and beyond their challenges. Colin is deeply inspired by Napoleon Hill, Earl Nightingale, and Bob Proctor.

Located in Grantham, he provides service on both sides of the Atlantic and takes great effort in accommodating the busy schedule of his clients.
